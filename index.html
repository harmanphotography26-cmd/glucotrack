Medical Disclaimer: This insulin dosing chart reflects a prescribed sliding scale provided by your doctor. Always verify doses with your healthcare provider before injecting. Never adjust insulin without medical guidance. In case of very high (>300) or very low (<70) readings, contact your doctor or go to the nearest hospital immediately. This app is for personal tracking only and does not replace professional medical advice.
